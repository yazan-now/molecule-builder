How to play

Build the target molecule
1
Pick an atom — tap Carbon, Nitrogen, or Oxygen in the dock. Hydrogens fill in automatically.
2
Place it — tap empty space for your first atom, then tap an existing atom to grow the chain.
3
Bond two atoms — with no atom selected in the dock, tap one atom then another to link them.
4
Match the name — the readout names your molecule live. Match the target to win.
5
Inspect in 3D — switch to View to orbit, pinch, and pan your creation.
